== Description ==

Feedburner Email Subscription gives your biggest fans another way to keep up with your content feed by placing an email subscription on your site. 
Easy to customize, just put your feed name and your ready to lunch.

<h4>Key Features & Options</h4>
<ul>
<li>No additional admin panel</li>
<li>Template function</li>
<li>Support shortcode</li>
<li>Extensive widget options</li>
<li>Widget intro and outro-text for your additional information, support HTML</li>
</ul>

== Installation ==

You can use the built in installer and upgrader, or you can install the plugin manually.
1. Go to the menu 'Plugins' -> 'Install' and search for 'Feedburner Email Subscription'
2. Click 'install'.
